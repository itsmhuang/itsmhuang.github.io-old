Portfolio

Womentum

JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX

WORK IN PROGRESS. Inspired by Momentum, Womentum is a Chrome extension that provides daily doses of information about inspirational woman in various industries as well as career and growth opportunities. Each day, the extension showcases a woman of note whenever the user open a new tab in their internet browser. Users can also click on social links to visit the woman's online pages, and click on a button to see career opportunities at their company. The web app is built by requesting HPE Haven on Demand APIs (Find Similar, Text Extract) with a cross domain AJAX call.
